Water Crisis 2050 : A World in Thirst

Context

By 2050, 1 in 4 people will face water scarcity.
Today, over 2 billion people already lack access to safe drinking water.
The most affected regions include the Middle East, North Africa, and India.

This project seeks to illustrate the urgent reality of global water scarcity through data.
By visualizing the scale of the crisis and highlighting regional disparities, the goal is to raise awareness about the preciousness of water and the need for sustainable solutions to preserve this vital resource for future generations.
